The App may display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect data such as:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Logs generated on your device (including crash logs) are not automatically sent to the Developer. They are only provided if you voluntarily contact the Developer and share them.
